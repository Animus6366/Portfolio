About Me

My Services

Here are some of the services I offer to help you secure your digital presence.

Cybersecurity Consulting

I provide expert advice on securing your systems, from small businesses to large enterprises. My services include risk assessment, policy development, and security audits to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities.

Penetration Testing

I simulate cyberattacks to find weaknesses in your network and applications before a real attacker does. This service helps you identify and fix security flaws to prevent data breaches.

Network Security

I design and implement secure network architectures, including firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and VPNs. My goal is to protect your network infrastructure from unauthorized access and cyber threats.

Security Training

I offer customized training programs to educate your staff on cybersecurity best practices. A well-informed team is the first line of defense against phishing, social engineering, and other attacks.
